About Prey Mooncrash Bingo

Objectives on cards only have to be done once, and you are free to reset the simulation as much as you want.

To Find a character is to get the sim points for placing your reticule on the corpse at close range.

Acquiring a weapon requires holding that weapon in your hands, this can be by either finding one or crafting one.

Prey Mooncrash bingo board is based on the test bingo board by Dessyreqt and the SRL Bingo v4 by Cosmo.
